Developer Account#

Get started with Pumble developer tools.

Start building add-ons for the Pumble app, the team communication tool that helps businesses stay connected and organized. Whether you're an individual developer or a company, Pumble provides everything you need to bring your add-ons to life, from setup to scaling in the CAKE.com Marketplace.

Create Developer Account#

Before publishing your first Pumble add-on, you need a CAKE.com developer account. Here's how to create one:

  1. Go to the CAKE.com developer page
  2. Click Sign up and enter your email address
  3. Verify your email (within 24 hours)
  4. Fill in your name, password, and accept the Terms of Use
  5. Once completed, you'll get access to your developer dashboard

Use your account to:

  • Build and manage your add-ons
  • Publish and maintain versions in the Marketplace

Publish Your First Add-on#

With your developer account set up, you're ready to publish your Pumble add-on:

  1. Log in to your developer account
  2. Go to the Add-ons page
  3. Click Create Add-on and follow the two-step form:

Step 1 – Basic Info:

  • Add icon
  • Name
  • Manifest URL
  • Choose Pumble as the product

Step 2 – Listing Info:

  • Provide a description
  • Category
  • Privacy policy
  • Optional terms
  • Visuals (images, demo video, etc.)

Once completed, submit for review. The review process typically takes up to two weeks.
